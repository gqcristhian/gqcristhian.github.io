About me

I am a Mexican PhD student at UT Dallas working in cosmology. I am interested in various topics in cosmology, astrophysics and particle physics. I did my undergraduate studies in Mexico where I worked on theoretical aspects of general relativity and cosmology and did several internships, around the country and also one in U.S. Then I came to Dallas to do my PhD in Physics with research in the area of cosmology.
My research has moved around different topics, but a brief list can be: Cosmological and dark energy parameter inference, modified gravity, 3x2-pt analysis, baryon acoustic Oscillations, among others. . However, I like to explore different topics inside cosmology that turn out to be appealing to me.
