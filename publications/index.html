Cristhian Garcia-Quintero

Physics PhD student at University of Texas at Dallas

I am a Mexican PhD student at UT Dallas working in cosmology. We are interested in testing GR searching for modifications to gravity at large scales. Following this aspect, I am one of the current developers of the ISiTGR code. Additionally, we are interested in quantifying the inconsistency/consistency between cosmological data-sets and dark energy models.
